## Ute Finance Options

### 1. Secured Car Loan (Personal Use)
For utes used primarily for personal/recreational purposes.

**Features:**
- Lower rates than unsecured
- Ute as security
- 1-7 year terms

### 2. Chattel Mortgage (Business Use)
For utes used 50%+ for business. Popular with tradies and ABN holders.

**Features:**
- Potential tax deductions
- Claim GST on purchase
- Interest deductible
- Depreciation claims

### 3. Commercial Hire Purchase
Business acquires ute through hire agreement with option to purchase.

**Features:**
- Business use
- Tax deductible payments
- Own at end of term

### 4. Novated Lease
Salary sacrifice ute through employer.

**Features:**
- Pre-tax payments
- Potential tax savings
- Includes running costs

## Tax Benefits for Business Utes

### Instant Asset Write-Off
Eligible businesses may immediately deduct the full cost of utes used for business.

### GST Credit
With chattel mortgage, claim GST credit on purchase price.

### Interest Deductions
Finance interest is tax deductible for business-use vehicles.

### Depreciation
Claim depreciation over the ute's effective life.

**Note:** Tax benefits depend on business use percentage. Consult your accountant.

## 4x4 vs. 4x2 Finance

Both configurations finance equally, but consider:

### 4x4
- Higher purchase price
- Higher fuel costs
- Better resale value
- Required for off-road work

### 4x2
- Lower purchase price
- Better fuel economy
- Adequate for most tradies
- Lower insurance costs

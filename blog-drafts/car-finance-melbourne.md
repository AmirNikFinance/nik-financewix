## Melbourne-Specific Considerations

### Stamp Duty (Motor Vehicle Duty)
Victorian stamp duty on vehicles:
- $20,000 car: ~$600
- $40,000 car: ~$1,200  
- $60,000 car: ~$2,100

### TAC Charges
Transport Accident Commission charges are included in registration. Budget $500-$800+ annually depending on vehicle type.

### Parking Permits
Inner Melbourne requires residential parking permits. Factor in permit costs for inner-city living.

### Congestion & PT
Consider if you really need a car for inner-city living. But if you do, we'll get you the best finance.

## Sydney-Specific Considerations

### Parking Costs
Factor in Sydney's parking costs when budgeting. Smaller cars can save on parking fees in the CBD.

### Tolls
If you use Sydney's toll roads (M2, M4, M5, Harbour Bridge, etc.), factor $2,000-$5,000+ annually into your budget.

### Stamp Duty
NSW stamp duty on vehicles varies by price and type. Budget approximately:
- $20,000 car: ~$600
- $40,000 car: ~$1,200
- $60,000 car: ~$1,800

### Green Slip (CTP)
Compulsory Third Party insurance in NSW varies by vehicle and suburb. Budget $400-$700+ annually.

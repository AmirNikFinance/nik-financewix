## FBT Exemption for EVs

### What It Means
Electric vehicles under the fuel-efficient vehicle threshold ($91,387 in 2024-25) are FBT-exempt when acquired through a novated lease.

### Who Benefits
Employees salary sacrificing an EV through their employer.

### The Savings
FBT exemption can save $5,000 - $15,000+ per year compared to a petrol equivalent.

### Eligible Vehicles
- Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs) ✅
- Plug-in Hybrids (PHEVs) ✅
- Hydrogen Fuel Cell ✅
- Standard Hybrids ❌

## EV Finance FAQs

### Can I finance a used EV?
Yes. Used EV financing works like any used car. Check battery warranty status.

### Does battery degradation affect resale/finance?
Yes. Lenders and buyers consider battery health. Most EVs retain 80%+ capacity for 8+ years.

### Are EVs more expensive to insure?
Sometimes slightly higher due to repair costs and parts availability. Shop around.

### Can I finance home charger installation?
Some personal loans can cover this. Or include in your overall EV budget.
